The human back is made up of a complicated structure that consists of muscles, ligaments, tendons, disks, and bones, all these structures work together to support the body and enable us to move around. Malfunctioning of any of these components can lead to back pain. Strain, medical conditions, and poor posture are a few reasons for back pain.

Back pain usually resolves with bed rest and home remedies, usually with gentle massage or exercise but sometimes medical treatment is necessary that generally includes some common painkillers, NSAIDS.
